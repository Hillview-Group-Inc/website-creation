Our Story

About Hillview Group

Closing the digital gap for local trades with affordable, professional web solutions.

Our Mission

Helping Underserved Trades Compete in a Digital-First World

We started Hillview Group because we saw a problem: incredible local tradespeople—cleaners, landscapers, HVAC technicians, handymen, and more—were losing business simply because they lacked a professional online presence.

Many are amazing at their craft. They show up on time, do quality work, and build great relationships. But when potential customers search online, they find competitors with polished websites while these hardworking professionals remain invisible.

Our mission is simple: close that gap affordably. We believe every legitimate business deserves to look established and credible online, regardless of their technical skills or budget size.

Built on Experience & Empathy
